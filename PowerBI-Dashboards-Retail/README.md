# Power BI Dashboards — Retail Business Intelligence

**Period:** October 2025 – March 2026  
**Stack:** Excel · Power BI · Power Query · DAX

---

## Overview

Designed and delivered business intelligence dashboards for two 
retail clients in Sogamoso and Nobsa, Boyacá, Colombia.
Both projects involved data modelling, ETL logic in Power Query,
DAX measures, and visual design for non-technical business owners.

---

## Project A — La Ruana de Mi Pueblo

**Client:** Artisan ruana store, Nobsa, Boyacá  
**Period:** October 2025 – February 2026

### Problem
The store had no visibility over sales patterns, product performance,
or category distribution across 19 products and 5 categories.

### Solution
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard connected to an Excel dataset
with 200 sales records. Applied Power Query for data cleaning and
DAX for calculated measures.

### Results
- Business analysis reduced from **2 hours to 10 minutes**
- Identified 80/20 product distribution for strategic decision-making

---

## Project B — Lizzmore

**Client:** Children and adult clothing store, Sogamoso, Boyacá  
**Period:** January 2025 – March 2026

### Problem
The store operated across multiple sales channels — physical store,
Instagram, WhatsApp Business, and Marketplace — but had no visibility
over channel performance or product category revenue distribution.

### Solution
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with 350 sales records,
30 products, and 6 categories. Separated digital channels for
independent analysis using Power Query and DAX measures.

### Results
- Identified that the **children's clothing segment (0–12 years) 
  represents 32% of total revenue**
- Detected peak sales periods in May and December
- Client shifted from intuition-based to data-driven inventory 
  and marketing decisions
